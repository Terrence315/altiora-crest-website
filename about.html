About Us

Built to produce excellent, disciplined, and globally prepared learners.

Altiora Crest Academy is envisioned as a values-driven Zimbabwean high school where academic excellence, leadership, and innovation are developed together.

Our story

Altiora Crest Academy was conceived to answer a clear need: a school that combines high academic standards, structured discipline, strong moral formation, and future-ready skills for Zimbabwean learners.

The academy’s design emphasizes measurable learner progress, teacher accountability, consistent parent communication, and a student culture rooted in aspiration, responsibility, and service.

Vision

National reputation, global outlook

To become one of Zimbabwe’s leading high schools for academic distinction and whole-person education.

Mission

Strong minds, strong character

To equip students with knowledge, discipline, confidence, and practical skills for life, university, and leadership.

Our core values

Excellence

We pursue high standards in scholarship, conduct, and service.

Integrity

We do what is right, even when it is difficult or unseen.

Discipline

We build strong habits, consistency, and accountability.

Leadership

We prepare learners to influence others with humility and courage.

School culture

Calm classrooms, respectful conduct, punctuality, neatness, and purposeful routines create an environment where learning thrives.

Parent partnership

We believe the most successful schools work in active partnership with families through regular communication and shared expectations.

Leadership formation

Students are developed through prefect structures, clubs, public speaking, service projects, and mentorship.

What families can expect

  • Qualified, committed teachers
  • Consistent assessment and reporting
  • Safe, structured learning environment
  • Balanced academic and character development
  • Strong emphasis on communication and accountability
