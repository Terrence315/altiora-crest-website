About the academy

Built for families who want more than just school fees and uniforms.

Altiora Crest Academy is designed as a high-standard Zimbabwean high school that blends local relevance with global ambition. We exist to nurture confident, ethical, and academically excellent learners who can compete anywhere while remaining grounded in values.

Our model is intentionally practical: outstanding teaching, disciplined routines, modern learning tools, and a strong partnership between school and home.

Admissions

Simple, transparent entry process

  1. Enquire: Request the prospectus and application pack.
  2. Apply: Submit the completed form and supporting documents.
  3. Assessment: The learner completes the entry assessment and interview.
  4. Offer: Successful applicants receive an offer letter and fee schedule.

Who can apply?

Prospective learners for Form 1 to Form 4. A-Level admission will follow future expansion.

Is boarding available?

The initial launch model is a day school, with boarding planned in later expansion phases.

Merit and need-based support can be introduced once the school’s scholarship fund is established.
