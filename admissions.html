Admissions

Clear, professional admissions process for families and learners.

Use this page to know how to apply.

How admission works

  1. Step 1: Parent requests a prospectus or submits an enquiry.
  2. Step 2: Parent completes the application form and submits documents.
  3. Step 3: Learner sits for an entrance assessment and interview.
  4. Step 4: School issues outcome, offer letter, and payment instructions.

Documents to request

Birth certificate copy, recent report, transfer letter where applicable, passport photos, and parent contact details.

Entry levels

Initial intake may focus on Form 1 and Form 2, with future expansion to higher forms.

Assessments

English and Mathematics assessments are common entry-screening tools for high schools.
